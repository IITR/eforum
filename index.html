IIT Roorkee Enterprise Forum is a platform for IIT Roorkee alumni, be it current students or graduates to explore their entrepreneurial self by pitching their new fangled business ideas and startups to a panel of senior VCs and entrepreneurs for mentorship and incubation. This platform is an online only platform bringing the diverse IIT Roorkee alumni to help each other and mentor each other in building great businesses of tomorrow. The platform will be executed by way of an online broadcasted video conference where entrepreneurs will pitch from their individual location to a panel of mentors online in from on live online audiences.

Irrespective of your age, location and how evolved your startup is, the enterprise forum is a place for you. As you will always find peers, seniors and juniors who might have ideas that benefit you and your journey towards entrepreneurship.

Gain Mentorship
Get mentored by the some of the most successful IIT Roorkee entrepreneurs and VC and get invaluable one-to-one feedback.

Get Networked
Benefit from the extensive networks and contacts of the Roorkee alumni by putting your idea in front of a wide audience.

Get Incubation
Build close relationships with Roorkee mentors to incubate your business with the proper ingredients of strategy, skills and resourcing.

Investment
Enterprise Forum plays no part in the investment relations and negotiations between startups and possible funders. Further the Enterprise forum is a free service for the entrepreneurs. It is expected however that the VC/mentors may get interested in investment discussions with particular startups.
